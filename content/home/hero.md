**Geocomputation, open source tools, and data science for sustainable transport**

I develop open source software and methods for transport planning, with a focus on active travel, cycling uptake, and decarbonisation. Author of [Geocomputation with R](https://geocompr.robinlovelace.net/) and [Geocomputation with Python](https://py.geocompx.org/), and creator of tools including the [Propensity to Cycle Tool](https://www.pct.bike/), [stplanr](https://docs.ropensci.org/stplanr/), and [stats19](https://docs.ropensci.org/stats19/) used by transport planners worldwide.

My research spans geocomputation, spatial data science, and evidence-based transport policy. I lead the Transport Data Science module at the University of Leeds and collaborate with organisations including Active Travel England, Transport Infrastructure Ireland, and the Department for Transport.
