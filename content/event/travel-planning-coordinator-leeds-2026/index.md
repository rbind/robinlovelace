I'll be attending this free networking event for travel plan coordinators in Leeds on 6 May 2026.

The event is organized by the Leeds City Council Travel Planning team and brings together people working on travel plans across the city. It's a great opportunity to connect with others in the field and learn about local sustainable transport initiatives including:

- Betterpoints
- Beryl Bikes
- Northern Rail
- Residential MCard
- Enterprise Car Club
- Public transport ticketing

The event runs from 10am to 12pm at Merrion House (110 Merrion Way, Leeds LS1 1UR).

Registration is free via [Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/travel-planning-co-ordinator-event-leeds-tickets-1983857569394).
